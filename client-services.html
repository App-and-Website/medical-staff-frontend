Medical Staff

Client Services

Medical Staff Nursing Agency is a leading, nationwide provider of general and specialised nursing staff, doctors, and allied health professionals to facilities across Australia. High quality service provision and thorough policies and frameworks are at the core of our business operation, and as such, Medical Staff maintains ISO 9001 accreditation for its quality assurance and management.

For nearly 2 decades, Medical Staff Nursing Agency has been recruiting and supplying Nurses and Midwives to healthcare and community facilities across Australia. Medical Staff places healthcare professionals in public and private hospitals, aged care facilities, retirement villages, private clinics, universities, TAFE, schools, Medical centres, NDIS and disability services, Homecare, vaccination, to name a few key examples.

Medical Staff Nursing Agency prides itself in supplying experienced, highly skilled, and specialised Nursing staff across Australia.

The team of Directors and Management at Medical Staff use their combined expert knowledge of the public and private healthcare industry to empower staff to work to their best abilities.

Our mission is centred on providing a tailored and individualised service to each of our clients, with a deep understanding of their needs, requirements, and expectations for staffing provision.

Each Agency staff member arrives at their first shift ready to work and equipped with all the prior induction. Medical Staff believes in time and cost saving initiatives, including saving our hospitals from having to conduct orientations for new staff. With a competitive rate of charge, Medical Staff always aims to save you money while providing the highest quality of staff and customer service support.
